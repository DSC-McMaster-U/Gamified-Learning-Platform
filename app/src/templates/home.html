Welcome to Our Gamified Learning Platform

The McMaster GDSC team has developed a dynamic Gamified Learning Platform, offering students an interactive space to effortlessly learn and absorb new content

Teachers & Students

Distinct dashboards and accounts for teachers and students, enabling efficient interaction. Teachers manage courses, Lessons, and quizzes. Students access materials, submit work, and track progress.

Lessons

Interactive lessons where students can engage with educational content by watching instructional videos, reading detailed notes, exploring textbooks, and participating in gamified memorization exercises.

Quizzes

Challenging quizzes for students to assess their knowledge on various subjects. Teachers can use these quizzes to gauge their students' understanding and track their progress.

Ranking System

Students on the platform earn points by completing modules and quizzes, which helps them track their progress and stay motivated while learning. A leaderboard shows the best performing students.
